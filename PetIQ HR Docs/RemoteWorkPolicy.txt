PetIQ Remote Work and Telecommuting Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Policy Number: HR-310

PURPOSE

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements at PetIQ. Remote work is a privilege, not a right, and is granted based on business needs and job requirements.

ELIGIBILITY

Employees may be eligible for remote work if they meet the following criteria:

Completed at least 6 months of employment with satisfactory performance
Job duties can be performed effectively outside the office
Have demonstrated strong time management and self-discipline
Have reliable internet connection and appropriate workspace
Manager approval required
Positions NOT eligible for remote work:

Roles requiring physical presence (warehouse, shipping, reception)
Positions handling physical inventory or equipment
New hires during their first 6 months (except as specifically approved)
Employees on performance improvement plans
REMOTE WORK ARRANGEMENTS

Full-Time Remote:

Employee works remotely 5 days per week
May be required to come to office for meetings, events, or training
Must live within 50 miles of a PetIQ office or approved location
Hybrid Schedule:

Employee splits time between office and remote work
Minimum 2 days in office per week (typically Tuesday-Thursday)
Remote days must be consistent and pre-approved
Temporary Remote Work:

Short-term arrangements (e.g., recovery from injury, family emergency)
Approved on case-by-case basis for up to 30 days
Can be extended with manager and HR approval
WORKSPACE REQUIREMENTS

Employees working remotely must maintain:

Dedicated, quiet workspace free from distractions
Ergonomic setup (desk, chair, proper lighting)
Secure environment for confidential company information
High-speed internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload)
Backup power source recommended
Professional background for video calls
PetIQ does NOT provide:

Home office furniture
Internet service reimbursement for local employees
Utility cost reimbursement
Renovation or workspace modification costs
PetIQ PROVIDES:

Laptop computer and necessary software
Monitor, keyboard, mouse (upon request)
VPN access and security software
Cell phone or phone stipend for eligible positions
EQUIPMENT AND SECURITY

Company Equipment:

All company equipment remains property of PetIQ
Must be returned immediately upon termination or end of remote work arrangement
Employees are responsible for care and security of equipment
Lost, stolen, or damaged equipment must be reported immediately
No personal use of company equipment by family members
Security Requirements:

Use company VPN for all work activities
Keep all software and security patches up to date
Lock computer when not in use
No use of public WiFi for work without VPN
Confidential documents must be stored securely
Shred or securely dispose of physical documents
Report any security incidents immediately to IT
Data Protection:

Comply with all data privacy and security policies
No storing company data on personal devices
Use encrypted file sharing methods only
Follow clean desk policy - no confidential materials visible
WORK HOURS AND AVAILABILITY

Core Hours:

Remote employees must be available during core business hours: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM local time
Respond to emails and messages within 2 hours during core hours
Attend all required meetings via video conference
Keep Slack/Teams status updated
Flexibility:

Work can be performed outside core hours if needed to meet deadlines
Must coordinate with team for coverage
Any schedule changes require manager approval
Time Tracking:

Non-exempt employees must accurately track all hours worked
Use company time tracking system
Overtime must be pre-approved by manager
Take required meal and rest breaks
COMMUNICATION EXPECTATIONS

Video Presence:

Camera ON for all team meetings and client calls
Professional appearance required
Appropriate background or virtual background
Response Times:

Email: Respond within 4 hours during business hours
Slack/Teams: Respond within 1 hour during core hours
Phone: Answer or return calls within 2 hours
Meeting Participation:

Join meetings on time
Minimize background noise (use mute when not speaking)
Actively participate and engage
Test technology before important meetings
PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS

Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees:

Meet all deadlines and productivity goals
Maintain quality of work
Participate in team collaboration
Complete all required training
Respond promptly to communications
Attend required in-person meetings/events
Managers will:

Set clear goals and expectations
Conduct regular check-ins (at least weekly)
Provide feedback on performance
Monitor productivity and deliverables
Address performance issues promptly
COMPLIANCE AND LEGAL

Tax and Legal Considerations:

Employees must work from their approved location
Moving to a new state/location requires HR approval BEFORE relocating
May affect tax withholding and legal compliance
International remote work is not permitted without special approval
Workers' Compensation:

Remote workspace is considered extension of PetIQ office
Work-related injuries must be reported immediately
Workers' compensation coverage applies during work hours only
Liability:

PetIQ is not liable for injuries to family members or visitors
Homeowners/renters insurance is employee's responsibility
Maintain safe workspace free from hazards
REQUESTING REMOTE WORK

To request a remote work arrangement:

Discuss with your direct manager
Complete Remote Work Request Form
Submit to manager for approval
HR review and final approval
Sign Remote Work Agreement
IT setup and equipment assignment
Schedule start date
Forms available on employee portal under HR > Forms > Remote Work

APPROVAL PROCESS:

Manager reviews and approves/denies
HR reviews for policy compliance
IT assesses technical requirements
Final approval from Department Director for full-time remote
MODIFICATIONS AND TERMINATION

Remote work arrangements can be modified or terminated:

With 2 weeks' notice for business needs
Immediately for performance or policy violations
At employee request with 2 weeks' notice
When employee changes positions
During company-wide events or critical projects
Returning to Office:

Employee must return all equipment or bring to office
Resume in-office schedule as directed
Workspace will be assigned based on availability
EXPENSES AND REIMBURSEMENT

Eligible Expenses (with receipts and pre-approval):

Business-related software or subscriptions
Office supplies necessary for job duties
Shipping costs for returning equipment
Travel to office for required meetings (over 50 miles)
Non-Reimbursable:

Internet service (for local employees)
Utilities (electricity, heating/cooling)
Home office furniture
Printer/ink for personal convenience
Coffee, snacks, meals
Commuting to office (under 50 miles)
Submit expense reports through employee portal within 30 days.

MONITORING AND PRIVACY

PetIQ may monitor:

Company device usage and activity
Email and messaging communications
File access and data transfers
Login times and productivity metrics
Video meeting attendance and participation
Employees have no expectation of privacy when using company equipment or systems.

TRIAL PERIOD

All new remote work arrangements include a 90-day trial period:

Performance and communication will be evaluated
Arrangement may be modified or terminated
Employee may request to return to office
Successful completion leads to ongoing arrangement
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Can I work remotely while traveling?
A: Occasional remote work while traveling within the US may be approved for up to 2 weeks. International work requires special approval.

Q: What if my internet goes down?
A: You must either come to the office, use mobile hotspot, or take PTO. Extended outages should be reported to your manager immediately.

Q: Can I work from a coffee shop?
A: Only with VPN and for non-confidential work. Not recommended for calls with sensitive information.

Q: Do I need to take PTO for doctor appointments?
A: Remote employees follow same PTO policies as in-office employees. Short appointments may be made up within the same week with manager approval.

Q: What if I need to relocate?
A: Contact HR BEFORE relocating. Relocation may affect your remote work eligibility, compensation, and tax withholding.

POLICY VIOLATIONS

Violations of this policy may result in:

Verbal or written warning
Suspension of remote work privileges
Disciplinary action up to and including termination
Examples of violations:

Falsifying time records
Security breaches or policy violations
Poor performance or missed deadlines
Failure to maintain availability
Unauthorized location changes
Inappropriate use of company equipment
