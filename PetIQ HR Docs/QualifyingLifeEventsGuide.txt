PetIQ Benefits - Qualifying Life Events (QLE)

You may make changes to your benefits outside of Open Enrollment if you experience a Qualifying Life Event. You must notify HR within 30 days of the event.

QUALIFYING EVENTS INCLUDE:

Family Status Changes:

Marriage or divorce
Birth or adoption of a child
Death of a dependent
Child reaching age 26
Gaining or losing a domestic partner
Employment Changes:

Spouse gains or loses employment
Change from full-time to part-time (or vice versa)
Start or end of unpaid leave
Spouse's open enrollment period
Residence Changes:

Moving to a new service area
Change affecting insurance availability
Other:

Loss of other health coverage
Court order requiring coverage
Medicare/Medicaid eligibility change
Significant cost or coverage changes by your carrier
TO REPORT A QLE:

Email HR at benefits@petiq.com within 30 days of the event
Provide documentation (marriage certificate, birth certificate, divorce decree, etc.)
Complete benefits change request form
Changes effective date of event or first of following month
REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION:

Marriage: Marriage certificate
Divorce: Divorce decree
Birth: Birth certificate
Adoption: Adoption papers
Death: Death certificate
Loss of coverage: Letter from previous insurer
Address change: Utility bill or lease agreement
IMPORTANT NOTES:

You have 30 days from the date of the event to request changes
Changes must be consistent with the qualifying event
Not all life events allow changes to all benefits
Missing the 30-day deadline means waiting until next Open Enrollment
For questions, contact HR at benefits@petiq.com or ext. 2400.
