Document 4: FSA Enrollment Guide
Flexible Spending Account (FSA) Guide for PetIQ Employees

An FSA allows you to set aside pre-tax dollars for eligible healthcare or dependent care expenses.

Healthcare FSA - 2026 Maximum: $3,200

Eligible Expenses:

Co-pays and deductibles
Prescription medications
Dental and vision care not covered by insurance
Over-the-counter medications with prescription
Medical equipment and supplies
Dependent Care FSA - 2026 Maximum: $5,000

Eligible Expenses:

Daycare for children under 13
Before/after school care programs
Summer day camps
Adult dependent care (if dependent lives with you)
IMPORTANT: FSA funds are "use it or lose it." Up to $610 can be carried over to the next year for Healthcare FSA only. Dependent Care FSA does not allow carryover.

How to Enroll:

Log into PetIQ employee portal
Select "Benefits" > "Open Enrollment"
Choose FSA option and enter annual contribution amount
Submit by November 30, 2025

Document 6: Qualifying Life Events
PetIQ Benefits - Qualifying Life Events (QLE)

You may make changes to your benefits outside of Open Enrollment if you experience a Qualifying Life Event. You must notify HR within 30 days of the event.

Qualifying Events Include:

Family Status Changes:

Marriage or divorce
Birth or adoption of a child
Death of a dependent
Child reaching age 26
Employment Changes:

Spouse gains or loses employment
Change from full-time to part-time (or vice versa)
Start or end of unpaid leave
Residence Changes:

Moving to a new service area
Change affecting insurance availability
Other:

Loss of other health coverage
Court order requiring coverage
Medicare/Medicaid eligibility change
To Report a QLE:

Email HR at benefits@petiq.com within 30 days
Provide documentation (marriage certificate, birth certificate, etc.)
Complete change request form
Changes effective date of event or first of following month
For questions, contact HR at ext. 2400.
