PetIQ Benefits Eligibility Requirements

FULL-TIME EMPLOYEES (30+ hours/week)

Eligible for all benefits on first day of month following 30 days of employment
Healthcare, dental, vision, life insurance, 401(k), FSA accounts
PART-TIME EMPLOYEES (20-29 hours/week)

Eligible for 401(k) immediately
Prorated PTO benefits
May purchase healthcare at full cost
TEMPORARY/SEASONAL EMPLOYEES

Not eligible for healthcare benefits
May participate in 401(k) after 1 year of service
DEPENDENTS

Spouse or domestic partner
Children up to age 26
Proof of relationship required
ENROLLMENT PERIODS
New hires must enroll within 30 days of hire date. Current employees may only make changes during Annual Open Enrollment (November 1-30) or within 30 days of a Qualifying Life Event.

For questions about eligibility, contact HR at benefits@petiq.com or ext. 2400.
